Step 1 · Connect ClickUp
Paste your ClickUp personal API token. Get one at app.clickup.com/settings/apps.
Step 2 · Email (optional)
Connect any SMTP account so BrokerFlow can send warm follow-ups on your behalf. For Gmail, create an app password.
Step 3 · SMS (optional)
Add Twilio credentials to send broker reminder texts. console.twilio.com
Step 4 · AI drafts (optional)
Add an Anthropic API key so Claude can draft warm follow-ups tuned to your relationship history. Get one at console.anthropic.com.
